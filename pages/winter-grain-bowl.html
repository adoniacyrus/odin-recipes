Winter Grain Bowl

Description

The Winter Grain Bowl is a warm, hearty, and wholesome dish perfect for colder months. It combines nutrient-dense grains, roasted seasonal vegetables, and a flavorful dressing to create a satisfying and nourishing meal. Packed with fiber, protein, and color, it's both healthy and comforting.

Ideal as a lunch or dinner option, this bowl is easily customizable and great for meal prep. The combination of roasted squash, Brussels sprouts, and a tangy tahini or balsamic dressing makes it a go-to winter favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked farro or quinoa
  • 1/2 cup roasted butternut squash (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup roasted Brussels sprouts (halved)
  • 1/4 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup chopped kale or spinach
  • 2 tbsp dried cranberries or pomegranate seeds
  • 1 tbsp toasted pumpkin seeds (pepitas)
  • 1–2 tbsp tahini or balsamic dressing
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper (for roasting)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the cubed butternut squash and halved Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20–25 minutes or until golden and tender.
  2. Cook the farro or quinoa according to package instructions and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked grain, roasted vegetables, chickpeas, and chopped kale or spinach.
  4. Top with dried cranberries or pomegranate seeds and toasted pumpkin seeds.
  5. Drizzle with tahini or balsamic dressing just before serving.
  6. Serve warm or at room temperature for a cozy, nourishing meal.
