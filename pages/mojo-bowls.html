Mojo Bowls

Description

Mojo Bowls are a vibrant, nutrient-packed meal inspired by tropical flavors and wholesome ingredients. Typically served in a bowl, this dish features a base of rice or quinoa, topped with a variety of fresh vegetables, fruits, grilled proteins, and drizzled with a tangy mojo-style sauce made from citrus, garlic, and herbs.

Perfect for a refreshing lunch or dinner, Mojo Bowls are both customizable and satisfying, offering a balance of taste, texture, and nutrition in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice or quinoa
  • 1/2 cup black beans (rinsed and drained)
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped mango or pineapple
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup red cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup corn kernels
  • Grilled chicken, tofu, or shrimp (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the brown rice or quinoa according to package instructions and set aside to cool slightly.
  2. Prepare and chop all the vegetables and fruits: slice the avocado, shred the carrots, slice the red cabbage, and chop the mango or pineapple.
  3. If using, cook your protein of choice (grilled chicken, tofu, or shrimp) and season it with salt, pepper, and a bit of lime juice.
  4. In a bowl, start layering: add the rice or quinoa as the base, then top with black beans, veggies, fruits, and protein.
  5. Drizzle with your favorite mojo sauce or a citrus-garlic vinaigrette.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve immediately.
