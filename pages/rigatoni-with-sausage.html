Rigatoni with Sausage

Description

Rigatoni with Sausage is a hearty and comforting Italian pasta dish made with tender rigatoni noodles and savory Italian sausage. Simmered in a rich tomato-based sauce and finished with grated parmesan and fresh basil, this meal is both satisfying and simple to make.

Perfect for weeknight dinners or cozy gatherings, this dish brings bold flavors with minimal effort. The ridged rigatoni pasta holds onto the sauce beautifully, ensuring every bite is full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz rigatoni pasta
  • 1 lb Italian sausage (mild or spicy, casings removed)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh basil, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated parmesan cheese (for serving)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the rigatoni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the sausage and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned and fully cooked.
  3. Add chopped onion and garlic to the pan and sauté until softened and fragrant, about 3–4 minutes.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, red pepper flakes (if using), and season with salt and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 10–15 minutes to allow the flavors to develop.
  5. Add the cooked rigatoni to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Serve hot, topped with grated parmesan and chopped fresh basil.
